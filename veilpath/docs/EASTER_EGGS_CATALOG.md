# Easter Eggs & Pop Culture References Catalog

**Philosophy: Like World of Warcraft, hide references EVERYWHERE**

Subtle nods to Gothic literature, horror films, fantasy epics, witch media, music, and more. Rewards observant players, creates community moments ("Did you catch the Poe reference?"), adds depth and humor.

## Implementation Notes

**Subtlety Levels:**

1. **Obvious (10%)** - Die-hard fans catch immediately ("Quoth the Raven")
2. **Moderate (60%)** - Most players catch with some thought ("The Nine Masks")
3. **Deep Cut (30%)** - Only superfans or explorers find ("White Pony statuette")

**Legal Safety:**

- Never quote directly (lyrics, dialog)
- Transform references (rename, adapt, hint)
- Parody doctrine protects some uses
- Avoid trademarked names when possible
- References are homage, not theft

**Community Engagement:**

- Players LOVE finding easter eggs
- Creates shareable moments (screenshot and post)
- Wiki pages will catalog everything
- Drives engagement ("Did you find...?")
- Builds loyal fanbase
