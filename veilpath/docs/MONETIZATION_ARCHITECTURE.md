# VeilPath Tarot: Monetization Architecture

**Strategy:** Battle Pass + Rotating Shop + Premium Currency
**Inspiration:** Fortnite Crew, Destiny 2 Eververse, Genshin Impact

---

## Design Inspirations

### Narrative & Atmosphere

**Planescape: Torment / Tides of Numenera:**
- Heavy, philosophical text
- Strange, metaphysical world-building (The Veil, liminal spaces, consciousness)
- Weird NPCs with complex motivations (Luna and Sol as enigmatic guides, not generic angels)
- Existential themes: "What can change the nature of a reading?"
- Choice-driven, reactive interpretations
- Mature, introspective, confronting shadow aspects

**Fallout 1 & 2:**
- Dark, gritty atmosphere without being edgy
- Isometric perspective aesthetic for card layouts
- Post-apocalyptic spirituality (finding meaning in ruins of self)
- Dark humor mixed with sincere wisdom
- Choice-driven outcomes (quest paths diverge based on player reflection)
- Text-heavy, slow-paced, contemplative

**Hearthstone:**
- Clean, readable cosmetic rarity (Standard/Gilded/Artifact)
- Generous free-to-play model with premium options
- Seasonal content keeps economy fresh
- Cosmetics enhance without gating core experience

### Visual Aesthetic

**Renaissance Illuminated Manuscripts:**
- Gold leaf, intricate filigree (Gilded tier)
- Hand-lettered, ornate borders
- Rich, symbolic imagery

**Art Nouveau:**
- Flowing, organic patterns
- Nature motifs (moon phases, vines, celestial bodies)
- Elegant, decorative frames

**Gothic Fantasy:**
- Moonlit, twilight atmosphere (never fully dark, never fully light)
- Stone ruins, candlelit altars, ancient forests
- Purple/violet and gold color palette (Shadowlands signature)

## Revenue Streams

**Price:** $4.99/month (auto-renewing subscription)

**Instant Unlocks:**
- 500 Veil Shards immediately
- Current season's exclusive card back

- 300 bonus Veil Shards

**Seasonal Progression (50 Tiers):**
- Earn through quests, not purchases
- Free track: Basic rewards every 15-20 tiers
- Premium track: Cosmetics every 5 tiers
- Tier 50: Complete premium Major Arcana art set

**Weekly Rotation:** 6 items (resets Monday)

**Cosmetic Categories:**
- **Card Backs** (50-300 Veil Shards)
- **Tarot Tables** (100-500 Veil Shards)
- **PC Skins** (200-800 Veil Shards)
- **Spreads** (150-400 Veil Shards)

- **Premium Card Art** (100 per card, 1500 for full deck)
- **Animated Cosmetics** (500-1200 Veil Shards - Lottie/Rive animations)

### 3. Veil Shard Purchases (Premium Currency)
**Price Tiers:**
- 300 Shards: $2.99
- 800 Shards: $4.99 (60% bonus)
- 1,600 Shards: $9.99 (100% bonus)
- 3,500 Shards: $19.99 (145% bonus)
- 7,500 Shards: $39.99 (190% bonus)

**Psychology:**
- $4.99 tier = Battle Pass price (encourage subscription instead)
- $9.99 tier = "I want 2-3 cosmetics now"
- $19.99+ = whales, seasonal splurges

### 4. Moonlight (Soft Currency)
**Earning:**
- Daily login: 25 Moonlight
- Complete reading: 10 Moonlight
- Quest completion: 15-50 Moonlight
- Achievements: 100-500 Moonlight

**Spending:**
- Basic spreads (5-card, relationship): 25-50 Moonlight
- Unlock common cosmetics: 500-1000 Moonlight
- Convert to Veil Shards: 1000 Moonlight = 50 Shards (bad rate, but possible)

**Purpose:** Engagement currency, gives non-payers progression

## Rarity System (PUBLIC - Hearthstone-Style)

**Inspired by:** Hearthstone's Standard/Golden/Diamond tiers

Three simple, clear tiers that players understand instantly:

### Rarity Tiers

| Rarity | Visual Treatment | Availability | Veil Shard Price Range |
|--------|-----------------|--------------|------------------------|
| **Standard** | Clean, high-quality base art | Always available in shop rotation | 50-200 |
| **Gilded** | Animated, particle effects, shimmering borders | Weekly rotation, Battle Pass rewards | 300-800 |
| **Artifact** | Ultra-premium, 3D effects, exclusive animations | Limited events, seasonal exclusives, founder rewards | 1000-2000 |

### Design Philosophy

**Standard:**
- Beautiful Midjourney art, no compromises on quality
- Solid colors, thematic designs, seasonal variants
- "This is what most players will use and love"
- 70% of cosmetic pool

**Gilded:**
- **Gold leaf effects** - Shimmering metallic accents
- **Ornate filigree borders** - Intricate scrollwork and patterns
- **Almost 3D appearance** - Embossed depth, beveled edges, shadows
- Subtle animations (shimmer, glow pulses)
- Think: Renaissance illuminated manuscripts meets Art Nouveau
- "Premium, but attainable with Battle Pass progression"
- 25% of cosmetic pool

**Artifact:**
- **Full sprite animations** - Like Hearthstone legendary/diamond cards
- **Particle systems** - Flowing energy, ethereal wisps, floating symbols
- **Cinematic 3D effects** - Rotating elements, depth parallax
- **Animated characters/scenes** - NPCs, environments in motion
- Signature glow effects (purple/gold aura for Shadowlands)
- Sound effects on hover/equip
- "I was there" cosmetics with narrative weight
- 5% of cosmetic pool, many never return

## Shop Rotation Mechanics

### Daily Shop (4 Slots)
**Slot 1:** Standard (70% of Standard pool)
**Slot 2:** Standard (30% of Standard pool, seasonal/themed)
**Slot 3:** Standard or Gilded (80/20 weight)
**Slot 4:** Gilded (rotating through Gilded pool)

Resets at midnight UTC

### Weekly Shop (6 Slots)
**Slots 1-2:** Standard (curated thematic pairs, e.g., matching card back + table)
**Slots 3-4:** Gilded (premium cosmetics)
**Slot 5:** Gilded (animated, high-demand items)
**Slot 6:** Featured (could be Artifact during special events, otherwise top-tier Gilded)

Resets Monday 00:00 UTC

### Shop Rotation Weights by Tier

| Tier | Daily Appearance Chance | Weekly Appearance Chance | Returns to Shop |
|------|------------------------|-------------------------|-----------------|
| **Standard** | 85% of slots | 33% of slots | Every 7-14 days |
| **Gilded** | 15% of slots | 60% of slots | Every 21-30 days |
| **Artifact** | Never in daily | 7% of slots (special events only) | Rarely or never |

### Seasonal Shop (Event-Based)
- Winter Solstice (December)
- Spring Equinox (March)
- Summer Solstice (June)
- Autumn Equinox (September)

Features Artifact-tier cosmetics, limited-time bundles

### "Moon's Blessing" Flash Sales
Random 24-hour events (2-3 per month):
- 50% off one premium cosmetic
- Bundle deals (card back + table + PC skin)
- Legendary item guaranteed in daily shop

- Month 2 (February): Refine and finalize
  - Upscale selected concepts
  - Create variations for rarity tiers
  - Export final assets (PNG, 2048Ã—2048 for card backs, 4096Ã—2048 for tables)

- Month 3 (March): Implement in app
  - Add to asset pipeline
  - Configure shop rotation pools

- Month 4 (April): Launch season

**Evergreen Content:** Always maintain 200+ cosmetics in rotation pool

### Database Schema (Phase 5)

```sql
CREATE TABLE cosmetics (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  cosmetic_type TEXT NOT NULL, -- 'card_back', 'table', 'pc_skin', 'premium_deck'
  name TEXT NOT NULL,
  description TEXT,

  -- Rarity (shown to user)
  rarity TEXT NOT NULL CHECK (rarity IN ('standard', 'gilded', 'artifact')),
  shop_weight FLOAT DEFAULT 1.0, -- rotation probability multiplier

  -- Pricing
  veil_shard_price INTEGER NOT NULL,
  moonlight_price INTEGER, -- if also earnable

  -- Availability
  is_battle_pass_exclusive BOOLEAN DEFAULT false,
  season_id TEXT, -- which season it belongs to
  available_start TIMESTAMP,
  available_end TIMESTAMP, -- for limited-time cosmetics

  -- Assets
  asset_path TEXT NOT NULL,
  preview_image_url TEXT,
  is_animated BOOLEAN DEFAULT false,

  -- Metadata
  created_at TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW(),
  updated_at TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW()
);

CREATE TABLE user_cosmetics (
  user_id UUID REFERENCES users(id),
  cosmetic_id UUID REFERENCES cosmetics(id),
  acquired_at TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW(),
  acquired_from TEXT, -- 'shop', 'battle_pass', 'gift', 'event'
  PRIMARY KEY (user_id, cosmetic_id)
);

CREATE TABLE user_equipped_cosmetics (
  user_id UUID PRIMARY KEY REFERENCES users(id),
  equipped_card_back UUID REFERENCES cosmetics(id),
  equipped_table UUID REFERENCES cosmetics(id),
  equipped_pc_skin UUID REFERENCES cosmetics(id),
  equipped_premium_deck UUID REFERENCES cosmetics(id),
  updated_at TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW()
);

CREATE TABLE shop_rotations (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  rotation_type TEXT NOT NULL, -- 'daily', 'weekly', 'seasonal'
  slot_index INTEGER NOT NULL,
  cosmetic_id UUID REFERENCES cosmetics(id),
  rotation_start TIMESTAMP NOT NULL,
  rotation_end TIMESTAMP NOT NULL,
  discount_percent INTEGER DEFAULT 0
);
```

## Key Design Principles

### 1. Clear, Simple Rarity Tiers
Three tiers players instantly understand:
- **Standard** = Beautiful, accessible
- **Gilded** = Premium, animated, gold leaf
- **Artifact** = Ultra-rare, legendary status
- No confusion, no 7-tier complexity
- Every tier has high-quality art (no filler/trash)

### 2. FOMO Without Predatory Practices
- Daily/weekly rotations create urgency
- But cosmetics eventually return
- Moon Keys let players unlock past seasons (anti-FOMO mechanic)
- No loot boxes, no gambling

### 3. Earn OR Buy (Hybrid Model)
- Battle Pass: Earn through quests (engagement)
- Shop: Buy instantly (whales, impulse)
- Moonlight: F2P can slowly earn cosmetics
- Everyone feels progression, whales feel special

### 4. Seasonal Content Keeps It Fresh
- 50-60 new cosmetics every 3 months
- Always something new in rotation
- Prevents stale economy
- Midjourney makes production cheap

### 5. Cosmetics Enhance, Don't Gate
- All spreads functionally available (Moonlight or small Shard cost)
- Cosmetics are pure expression, not power
- Premium card art doesn't give "better" readings
- Ethical monetization
