## ADVANCED TECHNIQUES

### 1. Consistency with `--cref` (Character Reference)

Once you have a perfect Luna or Sol portrait:
```
/imagine Luna giving oracle reading --cref [URL of original Luna image] --cw 100 --v 6
```
This keeps Luna looking consistent across multiple scenes.

### 2. Style Reference with `--sref`

Lock in your perfect tarot card style:
```
/imagine [new card concept] --sref [URL of perfect tarot card] --v 6
```

### 3. Remix Mode for Variations

Enable Remix mode in settings, then:
- Generate perfect "Ace of Wands"
- Click "Vary (Subtle)" or "Vary (Strong)"
- Change prompt to "Two of Wands" while keeping style

### 4. Upscaling for Print Quality

After generating:
1. Click "Upscale (2x)" or "Upscale (4x)"
2. For tarot cards: Upscale to 8K for print-ready quality
